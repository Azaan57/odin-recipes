Roast Chicken Recipe

Back to homepage

Description

A staple comfort food, featuring golden, crispy skin and succulent tender meat. It is often seasoned with herbs, lemon, and garlic and roasted until the juices run clear.

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken,
  • Butter,
  • Salt,
  • Black pepper,
  • Lemon,
  • Garlic,
  • Thyme/Rosemary

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400f
  2. Prepare the pan
  3. Prepare the chicken
  4. Roast
  5. Rest and serve
